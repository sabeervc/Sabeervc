About

I am Sabeer VC, a researcher specializing in the economics of international trade and migration. My doctoral work at CDS, affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru University, focuses on uncovering how migration flows shape and are shaped by global trade patterns.

Alongside my PhD, I serve as a Research Associate in the Trade Analytics division at the Indian Institute of Foreign Trade (IIFT), New Delhi — contributing to applied research on trade policy and economic development.

I am passionate about translating rigorous academic inquiry into insights that inform migration and trade policies with real economic development outcomes.

Research

Doctoral Research · Ongoing
International Migration & International Trade: Exploring the Nexus
Investigating how migration flows create trade linkages through networks, preferences, and institutional channels — with a focus on South Asia and the Gulf corridor.
Trade Policy · IIFT
Trade Analytics & Policy Research
Applied research on India's trade performance, export competitiveness, and policy implications at the Trade Analytics Division of IIFT Delhi.
Development Economics
Migration, Remittances & Development
Examining how remittance flows and migrant networks influence economic development outcomes in sending countries, particularly Kerala and South India.
Trade Dynamics
